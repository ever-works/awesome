## Overview

Awesome-Awesome-LLMs is a lighthouse for the LLM ocean, indexing high-quality awesome lists with ratings, covering training, multimodal, agents, infra, and more.

## Key Features

- **40+ Categories**: Pre-training, multimodal, agents, RAG, evaluation, security, etc.
- **Quality Ratings**: ★★★★★ based on community value.
- **Statistics**: 200+ lists, 500K+ stars, 10K+ papers.
- **Use Cases**: Researchers (trending topics), Developers (infra/tools), Students (courses), Practitioners (prompts/eval).
