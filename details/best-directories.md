## Overview

Meta-directory rating and organizing online directories by niches.

## Features

- Ratings and comparisons
- Category/tags exploration
- Filtering/sorting
- Trending highlights
- Owner submissions
- Newsletter updates

## Use Cases

- Directory comparison
- Niche discovery (AI, dev, finance)
- Research and submission
